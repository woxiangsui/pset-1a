Who is Mafumafu?

Mafumafu (まふまふ) is an utaite. But what is an utaite? According to the Utaite Wiki, an utaite "is a Japaense term for people who cover previously released songs and post them on Nico Nico Douga and Youtube under the uttattmita category". Mafumafu is well known for his high-ranged voice, many of his originial songs are written with high notes. Mafumafu's recent cover of Android Girl showed his ability to sing high notes without straining his voice. At the same time, Mafumafu is able to sing with a low raspy voice. Mafu's original song, GHOST featuring Nqrse displays his rapping abilities as well his vocal range.
