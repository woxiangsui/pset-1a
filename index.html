Who is Mafumafu?

Mafumafu (まふまふ) is an utaite. But what is an utaite? According to the Utaite Wiki, an utaite "is a Japaense term for people who cover previously released songs and post them on Nico Nico Douga and Youtube under the uttattmita category". Mafumafu is well known for his high-ranged voice.
